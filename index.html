Crystal Collector!

Rules of the Game

In the "Match the Number" box there will be a random number, betweeen 19 and 120, displayed.

Below that are 4 crystal buttons; each has a different value between 1 and 12.

When you click on a cyrstal the value amount will be added to your total score.

You can click on a crystal as many times as you like.

The game is won by getting your total score to equal the random number.

If you go over the amount you lose the game.

Then you can restart again with a new random number and new values on each crystal!

Good Luck!!!
